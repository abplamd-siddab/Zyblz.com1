1. Website Goal (Clarity First)

The website should answer within 5 seconds:

What Zyblz does

Who it is for

What problems it solves

Proof that it works (testimonials)

2. Homepage Structure (Recommended)
Hero Section (Above the Fold)

Headline (strong + simple):

We Build AI-Powered Software & Automations That Scale Your Business

Sub-headline:
Custom AI chatbots, workflow automation, and intelligent applications built for real business use cases.

Primary CTA:

“Book a Free AI Consultation”

Secondary: “View Our Work”

What We Do (Services Section)

Use icons + short descriptions, not long text.

Core Services:

AI Chatbots
Sales bots, support bots, WhatsApp bots, website bots

AI Workflow Automation
CRM automation, lead handling, follow-ups, reporting

Custom Software & Applications
Web apps, internal tools, SaaS products

AI Tool Integration
OpenAI, CRM, WhatsApp APIs, Zapier, Make, internal systems

Keep each service to 1–2 lines max.

Industries / Use Cases

Show practical relevance.

Examples:

Exporters & Trading Companies

Agencies & Service Businesses

Sales-Driven Teams

Operations & Back-Office Automation

SaaS & Startups

How Zyblz Works (Process Section)

Simple 4-step flow:

Understand Business Process

Identify Automation Opportunities

Build & Integrate AI Solution

Deploy, Train & Optimize

This builds trust and clarity.

Testimonials (Very Important)

This should be a dedicated, visible section.

Best practice:

Client photo (if possible)

Name + Company

1–2 line result-focused quote

Example format:

“Zyblz automated our lead handling and follow-ups. Our response time dropped by 70%.”
— Founder, Export Business

If you have multiple testimonials:

Show 3 on homepage

Link to “View All Testimonials”

Why Zyblz (Differentiation Section)

Answer: Why not a freelancer or generic agency?

Points you can highlight:

Business-first automation (not just tools)

Custom-built, not template-based

Real operational understanding

Ongoing support & optimization

Secure & scalable systems

Call to Action (End of Page)

Clear and direct:

Ready to automate your business with AI?
Button: Book a Free Call

3. Inner Pages You Should Have
Services Page

Each service explained with:

Problem

Testimonials Page

All testimonials in one place.

About Zyblz

Mission

What kind of problems you solve

Team or leadership credibility (even minimal)

Contact / Book a Call

4. Design Direction (Important)

Dark + light neutral theme (AI feel)

Clean fonts (Inter, Poppins, etc.)

Minimal animations

No clutter

Focus on trust + clarity, not fancy graphics
